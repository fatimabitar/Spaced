How it works

getting started is fast & easy

Spaced uses Bluetoth to locate and monitor devices/people near you. All you have to do is open the app, adjust your settings and choose a safety raduis you are comfortable with and the app does the rest!

Turn on Bluetoth and set your safe raduis

Allow location services(Android specific)

Turn on notifications so you can be notified when people are too close

Start tracking nearby devices so you can stay a safe distance

People nearby

Design your inner circle

We know you don't want to be notified every time your partner or your child walks in the room with their phone. To ignore a safe device/person, tap the tray at the bottom of the homescreen, find the device of your co-inhabitant and press the "ignore" button.

taking control

Choosing Your Settings

Choosing the settings that are right for you are easy. Just click the settings button in the upper left corner of the screen anytime to set or change your safety radius. This is also where you'll go for Bluetooth and other locations settings for this app.
